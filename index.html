An Easy-to-Understand Guide to Crypto and Blockchain

What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency transacted over the internet without the control of a central authority like a bank. All transaction records are stored in a transparent and secure distributed ledger technology called 'Blockchain.'

Blockchain: The Ledger that Records Everything

Blockchain is a technology where transaction records, or 'blocks,' are linked together like a chain. Once recorded, information is nearly impossible to tamper with, making it extremely secure and transparent.

Decentralization: A System Without a Central Authority

Cryptocurrencies are not controlled by any specific company or government but are run by the consensus of network participants. This allows individuals to have complete control over their own assets.
